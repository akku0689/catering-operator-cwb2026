# Privacy Statement — CateringOperator

## What Data We Collect
- Name
- Email address
- Event details (type, date, time, location)
- Dietary preferences
- Budget

## How We Use It
This data is collected solely to generate a catering 
plan for your event. It is not stored permanently, 
sold, or shared with any third parties.

## Data Storage
All conversations are processed through Microsoft 
Azure AI Foundry and Copilot Studio. Microsoft's 
privacy policy applies to data processed through 
these services.
